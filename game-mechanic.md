# Seven Knights Rebirth - Game Mechanics

## **1. Stats**  
*   **Physical Attack / Magic Attack:** *The higher a hero's Attack is, the greater the damage they deal is.*  
*   **Defense:** *The higher a hero's Defense is, the less damage they take.*  
*   **HP:** *HP decreases when taking damage from enemies, resulting in death when HP reaches 0.*  
*   **Speed:** *In combat, the order of basic attack is determined by comparing the Speed of all target. The higher the Speed, the earlier a hero attacks. The sum of each formation's Speed is also compared, and the formation with the highest total Speed takes the first skill turn.*  
*   **Crit Rate:** *The higher a hero's Crit Rate is, the more likly it is that a Critical Hit effect will occur. When a Critical Hit effect occurs, the enemy takes more damage.*  
*   **Crit Damage:** *The higher a hero's Crit Damage is, the greater the damage they deal with a Critical Hit effect is.*  
*   **Weakness Hit Chance:** *The higher a hero's Weakness Hit  Chance, the more likely it is that Weakness Hits will occur. When a Weakness Hit occurs, the enemy with the lowest HP ratio will be attacked first, and damage increases by 30%.*  
*   **Weakness Hit Damage:** *The higher a hero's Weakness Hit Damage is, the greater the damage they deal with a Weakness Hits is.*  
*   **Block Rate:** *The higher a hero's Block Rate is, the more likely they are to block damage from an enemy. When a Block occurs, a hero takes 50% less damage. If both a Critical Hit effect and a Block occur, they cancel each other out and the takes normal damage.*   
*   **Damage Taken Reduction:** *The higher a hero's Damage Taken Reduction is, the less damage they take.*  
*   **Effect Hit Rate:** *Effect Hit Rate decreases the target's Effect Resist when applying debuffs. Effect Resist cannot fall below 0.*  
*   **Effect Resistance:** *Higher Effect Resist decreases the chances of being afflicted with debuffs.*  

## **2. Heroes**
*   **Star Rank:** *Heroes possess a Star Rank ranging from 1⭐ to 6⭐. The higher a hero's Star Rank is, the greater their base **Attack**, **Defense**, and **HP** are.*  
*   **Grade:** *Heroes are classified by Grade into Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Legendary. Common heroes are natural 1⭐ heroes that can rank up to 3⭐. Uncommon heroes are natural 3⭐ heroes that can rank up to 5⭐. Rare and Legendary heroes are natural 4⭐ heroes that can rank up to 6⭐.*  
*   **Type:** *Heroes are divided into Attack, Magic, Defense, Support, and Universal Types. A hero's Type determines the distribution and growth of their base **Attack**, **Defense**, and **HP**.*  
*   **Level:** *Heroes can be level up to Lv.30 with increased **Attack**, **Defense**, and **HP** at each level.*  
*   **Enhance:** *Heroes can be enhanced up to +5 with increased **Attack**, **Defense**, and **HP** at each level.*  
*   **Transcend:** *Natural 4⭐ heores and above can transcend up to 12 times using duplicate heroes. Transcendence enhances skills or **stats**, and specific effects vary by hero. However, skill enhancement effects only apply to heroes rated 6⭐.*  
*   **Skills & Enhance:** *6⭐ heroes can have their skills enhanced. Skill enhancement grants the skill new effects or improves existing ones*  
*   **Potential:** *You can further increase the **Attack**, **Defense**, and **HP** stats of 6⭐ heroes with +2, +4, and +6 Transcend by enhancing their Potential. Each stats can be enhancing up to +10, +20, and +30 respectively to Transcendence.*  
*   **Equipment:** *Each hero has equipment slots for 2 Weapons, 2 Armors, and 1 Accessory. Equipping items further increases a their **Stats**.*

## **4. Formation**
*   In Team Formation, you can deploy heroes in specific positions. However, you cannot place duplicate heroes in a sigle team. Choose from four different formation to suit your strategy. Heroes in the front row have a higher chance of being attacked but recieve a Defense boost. Heroes in the back row have a lower chance of being attacked and receive an Attack Boost.
